Liverpool Oo Heshiis Hordhac Ah La Gaartay Daafac Ka Ciyaara Serie A.

Liverpool ayaa lagu soo waramayaa inay heshiis hordhac ah la gaartay Denzel Dumfries oo ay kaga soo qaadan doonto Inter Milan beeca xorta ah xagaaga soo aadan. Daafaca midig ee reer Holland ayaan wax heshiis ah u kordhin kooxdiisa Inter. Heshiiska uu Damfries kula jiro ayaa ku eg xilli ciyaareedkan. Inter waxey ka cabsi qabtaa daafaceeda in uu mar hore heshiis la gaadhay Liverpool. Dumfries waxa uu u ahaa ciyaaryahan muhiim u ah Inter, isagoo kula guuleystay horyaalka Seria A, laba Coppa Italia, halka uu gaaray finalka Champions League 2023. Liverpool ayaa wali rajaynaysa in ay sii haysato Trent Alexander-Arnold kaas oo uu heshiiskiisu dhacayo dhawaan laakiin Dumfries ayaa noqon doona mid wax ku soo kordhin kara hadii uu Arnold baxo. Liverpool ayaa sidoo kale xiiso u muujineysa saxiixa Martin Zubimendi kadib qaab ciyaareedkiisii ​​fiicnaa ee guushii Spain ee Euro 2024, laakiin ciyaaryahanka khadka dhexe ayaa doortay inuu sii joogo Real Soceidad. Dhanka kale, saxiixii ugu weyn ee uu sameeyay macalinka cusub ee Liverpool Arne Slot intii lagu jiray suuqa wuxuu ahaa Federico Chiesa oo Juventus kaga soo biiray 10 milyan ginni. Iyadoo ay jiraan caqabadahaas, Liverpool ayaa u muuqata inay isku diyaarinayso xagaaga soo socda iyadoo Dumfries ay tahay dooqa ay doorbidayaan. Dumfries, oo 29 jir ah, ayaa 60 kulan u saftay Netherlands, waxaana uu caan ku yahay qaabkiisa weerarka isaga oo ah daafac ah. Liverpool ayaa sidoo kale ka shaqaynaysa sidii ay heshiis kordhin ugu samayn lahayd ciyaartoydeeda muhiimka ah sida Mohamed Salah iyo Virgil van Dijk.

Waa Kee Ciyaaryahanka Maskaxdooda Kusoo Dhacaya Haddii Ay Maqlaan Musi-ga Champions League-ga | Haaland Iyo Xiddigo Kale Ayaa Ka Jawaabay.

Dhowr xiddig oo kubbadda cagta ah, oo ay ku jiraan Erling Haaland iyo Virgil van Dijk, ayaa magacaabay Cristiano Ronaldo markii la waydiiyay su’aal Champions League-ga ah. Xiddigaha ugu waaweyn kubada cagta sida Haaland, Van Dijk, Phil Foden, Kevin De Bruyne iyo Trent Alexander-Arnold ayaa la waydiiyay in ay magacaabaan ciyaaryahanka ugu horeeya ee maskaxdooda ku soo dhaca marka ay maqlaan Astaanta Champions League-ga oo ay dhamaantood wada magacaaben xidigii hore ee Real Madrid iyo Manchester Cristiano Ronaldo. Ronaldo waa mid ka mid ah ciyaartoyda loogu jecel yahay kubadda cagta taariikhda Champions League, isagoo ku guuleystay shan jeer horyaalka, afar uu la qaaday Los Blancos iyo hal mar oo uu la qaaday Red Devils. Waxa uu ka soo muuqday rikoor 183 kulan ah oo uu dhaliyay 140 gool, waana ciyaaryahanka ugu badan ee taariikhda tartankan dhaliyay. Guulihiisa gaarka ah waxaa dhawaan aqoonsaday xiriirka UEFA markii ay ku siiyeen abaalmarin gaar ah intii lagu jiray isku aadka Champions League 2024-25. 39 jirkaan ayaa sii wada gool dhalinta isagoo todobo jeer shabaqa soo taabtay sideed kulan oo uu u saftay Al-Nassr xilli ciyaareedka socda.
