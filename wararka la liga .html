Lamine Yamal Oo Iska Diiday In Lala Barbardhigo Laacibkaan.

Xiddiga da’da yar ee Barcelona, ​​Lamine Yamal, ayaa saameyn weyn ku yeeshay kooxdiisa tan iyo markii uu saftay kulankiisii ​​​​ugu horreeyay asagoo keliya jira 16 sano, inkastoo uu yahay mid ka mid ah xirfadleyda kubbadda cagta, agaasimihii hore ee Barcelona Jordi Cruyff ayaa sharraxay sababta ay xilli hore u tahay in Yamal lala barbardhigo “Greatest of All Time”kan ugu fiican kubadda cagta (GOAT) Lionel Messi. Inkastoo Yamal uu muujiyay bandhigyo wax ku ool ah, Cruyff ayaa tilmaamay in xiddigan uu weli yahay da ‘yar, ayna cadaalad darro tahay in lala barbar dhigo Messi, kaasoo soo qaatay wakhti aan caadi ahayn. Cruyff wuxuu sheegay in isbarbardhigga dhabta ah la samayn karo kaliya marka Yamal uu gaaro da’da 30 ka. Yamal qudhiisu waxa uu qiray in ay adag tahay in la gaarsiiyo heerka Messi, isagoo sheegay in ay sharaf u tahay in lala barbardhigo Messi. Waxa uu carabka ku adkeeyay in gaaridda darajada Messi ay tahay wax aan macquul aheyn. Hadda oo uu ka hoos ciyaarayo tababare Hansi Flick, Yamal ayaa muujiyay sida uu ugu faraxsan yahay xorriyadda uu Flick siiyo ciyaartoyda, taasoo uu aaminsan yahay inay kooxda ka caawinayso inay qaab ciyaareed fiican soo bandhigto. Waxa uu sidoo kale firfirconida jir ahaaneed ee ciyaartooyda xilli ciyaareedkan marka loo eego kii hore. Yamal iyo Barcelona ayaa isku diyaarinaya kulankooda xiga ee horyaalka La Liga ee ay la ciyaarayaan Sevilla.


Ronaldo Oo Cod Carro Leh U diray Xiddig Caan Ah.

Cristiano Ronaldo ayaa lagu eedeeyay inuu cod caro leh u diray xiddigii hore ee Real Madrid Antonio Cassano kadib markii uu dareemay ixtiraam darro ka timid hadalka Cassano. Cassano, oo muddo dheer ku soo dhex jiray kubadda cagta una soo ciyaaray kooxo ay ka mid yihiin Roma, AC Milan, iyo Inter Milan, ayaa dhowr jeer si cad u dhaleeceeyay Ronaldo. Hadal uu dhawaan soo saaray Cassano waxa uu ku sheegay in Ronaldo aanu garanayn sida loo ciyaaro kubbada cagta, isla markaana waxa kaliya ee uu diirada saaro ay tahay in uu gool dhaliyo. Waxa uu xitaa sheegay Cassano iney jiraan ilaa shan ciyaaryahan oo kale oo ay ku jiraan Lionel Messi iyo Ronaldo Nazario, kuwaas oo uu ku andacooday inay ka wanaagsanaayen Ronaldo. Sida laga soo xigtay Cassano, wuxuu helay qoraal aan toos ahayn oo ka yimid lambar Isbaanish ah (+34), kaas oo uu markiiba ogaaday inuu ka yimid Ronaldo laftiisa. Fariintan ayuu Ronaldo ku taxaabay dhamaan koobabkii iyo guulihii uu gaadhay intii uu ku jiray xirfadiisa ciyaareed isaga oo muujiyay sida uu uga cadhooday in magaciisa laga hoos mariyo xiddigo ay xifaaltamaan. Ronaldo ayaa qoraalka ku shaaciyay in Cassano keliya uu dhaliyay 150 gool,kuna guuleystay afar horyaal.waxaa kale uu usheegay farqiga u dhaxeeya labadooda. Cassano ayaa ka jawaabay oo u sheegay Ronaldo in arintu aysan ahayn koobab ama goolal, balse uusan gebi ahaanba jeclayn Ronaldo ciyaaryahan ahaan. Ma ahan markii ugu horeysay uu Ronaldo si adag uga jawaabo dhaleeceyn loosoo jeediyay. Horraan waxa uu u xannibay shabakadda TransferMarkt ka dib markii ay ku qiimeeyeen inuu ka hooseeyo inta uu aaminsan yahay inuu u qalmo. Xaaladan ayaa muujineysa sida uu Ronaldo ugu nugul yahay dhaleeceynta, gaar ahaan marka ay timaado taariikhda kubada cagta iyo guulaha shaqsiyadeed.


Kylian Mbappe Oo Lagu Eedeyay in Xulkiisa France Uu ka Doorbidayo Kooxdiisa Real Madrid, qaabkee?

Kylian Mbappe ayaa dhaleecayn kala kulmay taagerayaasha xulkiisa Faransiiska, kadib markii uu kasoo muuqan waayey xidigaha ku meteleya xulka Faraniiska ciyaaraha qaramada, isaga oo kusoo bilowday ciyaartii kooxdiisa Madrid guusha 2-0 ah kaga gaadhay Villareal, isaga oo 25 jirkani isku dayaya inuu helo qaabciyaareedkiisi ugu fiicnaa.

Sida laga soo xiganayo Cadena SER Mbappe ayaa heshiis kula galay tababaraha xulka Faransiiska Didier Deschamps inuu la sii joogo kooxdiisa Real Madrid si uu ugu soo noqdo qaabkiisa ugu fiican. Mbappe ayaa isbuucan la joogaya tabararaha Real Madrid ee taam ahaanshaha Antonio Pintus si uu u helo taam ahaanshiyo buuxa. 11-ka kulan ee ilaa hada uu u ciyaaray kooxda Real Madrid, Mbappe ayaa ku lug yeeshay 7 goal iyo 1 caawin, laakiinsee uu dareemayo inaanu weli u ciyaaraynin qaabkiisi ugu fiicnaa, xili Real Madrid ay wajahayso kulamo muhiim ah xulalka kadib, Celta Vigo oo horyaalka ah ka hor inta aanay la ciyaarin Borrusia Dortmund iyo Barcelona. Iyadoo la xasuusto in Mbappe uu gebi ahaanba seegay kulamadii isku diyaarinta xili-ciyaareedka ee Madrid isaga oo iska dhamaystay fasaxiixa kadib EURO, isaga oo kusoo laabtay waxyar ka hor uun ciyaarista UEFA Super Cup, Mbappe ayaa dhaawacyo ku seegay qaybo ka mid ah kulamadii Lille iyo Villareal.
