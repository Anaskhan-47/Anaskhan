Wararka suuqa kala iibsiga ciyaartoyda waxaan ku soo gudbinaa wararkii ugu danbeeyay ee suuqa, xanta wargeysyada Yurub ay ka qoreen ciyaartoyda kala wareegeysa. Waxaan ku dadaalnaa inaan idiin soo gudbino heshiis walba oo dhaca iyo wakhtiga uu dhaco


Juventus Oo Si Lama Filaan Ah Ugu Dhaqaaqaysa Halyeeyga Real Madrid Sergio Ramos.

Juventus ayaa lagu soo waramayaa inay ka fiirsaneyso saxiixa Sergio Ramos iyadoo kooxda Turin ay isha ku heyso halyeeyga Real Madrid ka dib markii Gleison Bremer uu ka dhaawacmay. Juve ayaa Masiibo ku dhacday ciyaartii Champions League ee ay la ciyaareen RB Leipzig, iyadoo Bremer uu dhaawac halis ah soo gaaray taasoo keentay inuu garoomada ka maqnaado inta ka dhiman xilli ciyaareedka. Dhaawaciisa ayaa dharbaaxo weyn ku ah Juventus, maadaama daafaca reer Brazil uu qeyb muhiim ah ka ahaa qorshaha taatikada ee Thiago Motta. Juventus ayaa ka fikiraysa saxiixa Ramos maadama ay 38 jirkan u arkaan dookh macquul ah oo ay ku xoojin karaan difaacooda marka uu maqan yahay Bremer. Daafaca dhexe ayaa koox la’aan ahaa tan iyo markii uu ka tagay Sevilla, waana suurtogal inuu aqbali u dhaqaaqista Juve. Inkastoo Ramos uu dalabyo ka helay Saudi Arabia, Major League Soccer, iyo heerka sare ee Brazil, weli ma uusan u ballan qaadin inuu aadayo mid ka mid ah horyaaladaas. Waxaa xusid mudan in Ramos uu iska diiday dalab uga yimid kooxda Sacuudiga ee Al-Ittihad ka hor inta uusan dib ugu biirin Sevilla sanadkii 2023-ka. Haddii uu hadda ogolaado ku biirista Juventus, waxay calaamad u noqon doontaa markiisii ​​​​ugu horreeyay ee uu Serie A ka ciyaaro, taasoo ka dhigeysa horyaalkiisii ​​seddexaad ee ugu weyn Yurub ka dib La Liga iyo Ligue 1.


Liverpool Oo Qarka U Saaran Inay Man United Ka Afduubato Daafaca Everton Ee Jarrad Branthwaite.

Liverpool ayaa laga yaabaa inay Manchester United kula tartanto saxiixa daafaca aadka loo qiimeeyo ee Everton Jarrad Branthwaite. Liverpool ayaa laga yaabaa inay dalab ka gudbiso Everton daafaceeda Jarrad Branthwaite isla bisha Janaayo. Manchester United ayaa qorsheyneysa inay u dhaqaaqdo xagaaga soo aadan, laakiin Liverpool ayaa laga yaabaa inay dalab halis ah ka gudbiso bilowga sanadka. United ayaa qorshaynaysa in ay dalab cusub ka gudbiso Jarrad Branthwaite xagaaga soo socda waxayna halis ugu jirtaa in ay ka afduubato kooxda ay xafiiltamaan ee Liverpool. “Bishii July, United ayaa 2 dalab ka gudbisay Branthwaite waxaa diiday Everton, oo ku dooneysa in ka badan 70 milyan oo ginni daafaca dhexe. United ayaa la filayaa inay mar kale isku daydo laakiin waa xagaaga soo socda, iyagoo wax ka badan 100 milyan ginni ku qarash gareeyay daafacyada Leny Yoro iyo Matthijs de Ligt. Dhaqdhaqaaq kasta oo suuqa kala iibsiga ah ee bisha Janaayo waxay u badan tahay inay diiradda saaraan weeraryahan cusub. Liverpool aad ayay ula dhacsan yihiin Branthwaite, waxaana laga yaabaa inay u dhaqaaqaan Janaayo iyo sidoo kale inay u soo bandhigaan Everton ku dhawaad ​​waxa ay doonayaan.


Xiddig Liverpool Ka Tirsan Oo doonaya Inuu Ku Biiro Bayern Munich…

Liverpool ayaa haatan hogaanka u haysa kala saraynta horyaalka Premier League-ga kadib markii ay ku bilaabatay xilli ciyaareedka cusub qaab ciyaareed fiican. Imaanshaha Arne Slot ayaa nolosha dib ugu soo celisay kooxda, wax ka maqnaa dhamaadkii xilli ciyaareedkii hore. 15 dhibcood oo ay ka heleen lixdii kulan ee ugu horeysay xilli ciyaareedkan, waxa ay ka sareeyaan kooxaha Manchester City iyo Arsenal ee horyaalka Premier League-ga. Su’aasha ku saabsan mustaqbalka qaar ka mid ah ciyaartoydooda ayaan wali saameynin xilli ciyaareedkooda. Mohamed Salah, Virgil van Dijk iyo Trent Alexander-Arnold iyo xiddigo kale oo Liverpool ah ayaa wajahaya mustaqbal aan la hubin oo kooxda ah. Sida laga soo xigtay The Sun goolhayaha Liverpool Alisson Becker ayaa isha ku haya inuu u dhaqaaqo kooxda Jarmalka ee Bayern Munich. Waxa ay isha ku hayaan in ay badalaan goolhayaha halyeeyga ah ee Manuel Neuer dhamaadka xilli ciyaareedkan waxana uu Alisson ka mid yahay musharixiinta ay u aqoonsadeen booska goolhayaha. Neuer oo 28 jir ah ayaa gaaray heerkii ugu dambeeyay ee xirfadiisa ciyaareed, waxaana labadii xilli ciyaareed ee u danbeysay uu soo maray arrimo dhaawac halis ah. Goolhayaha Jarmalka ayaa kaliya saftay 35 kulan Bundesliga labadii xilli ciyaareed ee ugu dambeeyay sababo la xiriira taam ahaanshihiisa. Qandaraaska uu ku joogo garoonka Allianz Arena ayaa lagu wadaa inuu dhaco dhamaadka xilli ciyaareedkan, iyadoo mustaqbalkiisa hawada sare uu ku jiro, kooxda Jarmalka ayaa Alisson u calaamadeysay bedelka macquulka ah ee Neuer. Liverpool ayaa xagaagan la soo wareegtay goolhaye Giogi Mamardashvili waxayna amaah ugu dirtay Valencia xilli ciyaareedkan. Goolhayaha reer Georgia ayaa kooxda iman doona xagaaga soo socda waxana uu kula tartami doonaa booska goolhayaha reer Brazil. Alisson wuxuu u ahaa adeege cajiib ah Reds, isagoo ka caawiyay inay ku guuleystaan ​​Premier League iyo Champions League. Bixitaankiisa ayaa wax weyn ku noqon doonta Liverpool hadii uu ka tago kooxda dhamaadka xilli ciyaareedkan.
