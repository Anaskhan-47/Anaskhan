Ciyaartoy Man United Ah Oo Shaaciyey Inuu Kooxda Ka Tagayo Dhamaadka Xili-Ciyaaredkan.

Ciyaartoy u ciyaara kooxda Man United ayaa sida lasoo tebinayo ka hadlay mustaqbalkiisa kadib bilaabasho aan fiicneyn oo United ku bilaabatay xili-ciyaaredkan. Wakhti xaadirkan Man United ayaa fadhida kaalinta 14aad kala saraynta Horyaalka, xili ay sidoo kale natiijoyiin aan fiicnayn ka heleen tartanka Europe League, United ayaa sidoo kale guul la’aan ah 5 kulan oo isxiga kadib guushii 7-0 ahayd ay ka gaadheen Barnsley. Natiijooyinkaas liita ee isxigxigay ayaa keenay in shaki la geliyo mustaqbalka tababareha Kooxda Man United Erik Ten Hag. Warar soo baxaya ayaa sheegaya in khadka dhexe ee Man United Christian Eriksen uusan weli wax wada hadal ah kala yeelan kooxda United heshiis mudo kordhin ah maadama heshiiskiisa hada uu dhacayo dhamaadka xili-ciyaareedkan. Fabrizio Romano oo la weydiiyey hadii uu Eriksen kula jiro United wadahadalo heshiis kordhin ah ayuu Romano ku jawaabay: “Maya, gebi ahaanba ma jiraan wax wadahadalo ah”. Christian Eriksen oo dhaleecayn kala kulmay isbuucan kabtankii hore ee Man United Roy Keane ayaa la filayaa inuu ka tegi doono geggida Old Trafford dhamaadka xili-ciyaareedkan.


Alan Shearer Oo Ammaan Kala Dul Dhacay Xiddig Kamid Ah Xiddigaha England.

Kabtankii hore ee England Alan Shearer ayaa sheegay in weeraryahanka Aston Villa Ollie Watkins ay tahay “inuu xanaaqo” ka dib markii lagu soo bilaabi waayay kulankii England ee UEFA Nations League ee Greece iyadoo Harry Kane uu dhaawac ku maqnaa. Tababaraha ku meel gaadhka ah ee Lee Carsley ayaa go’aansaday inuusan ku soo bilaabin Watkins wuxuuna isticmaalay shax aan lahayn weeraryahan toos ah. Watkins ayaa badel ku soo galay daqiiqadii 60-aad markaasoo England lagu lahaa 1-0. Shearer ayaa aaminsan in Watkins, oo qaab ciyaareed fiican kusoo bandhigay kooxdiisa, ay tahay inuu ka niyad jabo inuusan helin fursad uu ku soo bilowdo, gaar ahaan iyadoo Kane uu maqnaa. Shearer xitaa wuxuu soo jeediyay Watkins inuu kala hadlo tababaraha sidii uu u heli lahaa door bilow ah ciyaaraha soo socda. Gary Lineker, oo ah daadihiyaha podcast-ka The Rest Is Football, ayaa waydiiyay Alan Shearer in ciyaartoydu ay si dhab ah ula xiriiraan tababarayaasha, taas oo Shearer uu ku jawaabay in uu sidaas sameeyn jiray markii uu yaraa. Waxa uu sheegay in aanay waxba yeeleynin tababaraha in la wareysto oo lala hadlo, gaar ahaan marka taatikada tababaraha aanay shaqayn, sida kiiskii guul-darradii Giriigga. Shearer waxa uu qiray in Carsley uu shaqo adag hayo maadaama uu leylinayo ciyaartoy badan oo tayo leh oo aan la kala dooran karin, isaga oo is barbar dhigay xaaladdii madax xanuunka ahayd horraantii sanadkii 2000 markaas oo England heysatay Paul Scholes, Steven Gerrard iyo Frank Lampard.
