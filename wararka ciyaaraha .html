Wararka Ciyaaraha Maanta

ciyaaraha maanta

Wararka ciyaaraha maanta kala soco wararkii ugu danbeeyay ee kubada cagta gaar ahaa horyaalada ugu waa weyn qaarada Yurub. Natiijooyinka ciyaaraha, suuqa kala iibsiga ciyaartoyda iyo wararka xiisaha ah ee dunida kubada cagta ka dhaca.

Real Madrid oo Xidigo Muhiim Ah Dhaawac Uga Soo Laabanayaan Kadib Ciyaaraha Qaramada.

Kooxda reer Spain ee Real Madrid ayaa kulamadii u dambeeyay la dhibaatoonaysay dhaawacyo kala duwan, iyada oo dhibaatoyin kale oo taam ahaansho kusoo biireen kadib guushii ay ka gaadheen Villareal kahor intii aan la aadin ciyaaraha xulalka. Iyada oo dhaawacii cuslaa ee Carvajal kasoo gaadhay kulankaas keliya ay tabinayeen warbaahintu laakiinse isla kulankaas ay sidoo kale dhibaatooyin dhaawac kasoo gaadheen Vinicius Junior, Eder Militao iyo Andriy Lunin. Carlo Ancelotti ayaa filaya in badi ciyaartoyda dhaawaca ku maqan ay kasoo kabtaan ka hor kulanka ay la leeyihiin Celta Vigo, Andriy Lunin ayaa la filayaa inuu kaso kabto xanuun, sidoo kale Thibaut Courtois oo maqnaa 2-dii kulan ee ugu dambeeyay Real Madrid ayaa la filayaa inuu kusoo noqdo kooxda. Eder Militao iyo sidoo kale Vini Jr ayaa la filayaa inay kasoo kabtaan dhaawacyo fudud oo soo gaaray kulanki Villareal, xidig kale oo isagana la filaayo inuu kaso noqdo dhaawac ayaa ah Brahim Diaz. Real Madrid ayaa wajihi doontaa isbuuc muhiim u ah iyaga oo ciyaaraya kulamo adag, Celta Vigo oo ay ku xigaan ka hor taga BVB ee Champions League iyo kulanka ugu weyn horyaalka Spain ee Elclassico oo ay kooxda Barca isku wajihi doonaan isbuucyada soo aadan.


Koox Ka Dhisan Ruushka Oo Barcelona Kaga Guuleysatay Maxkamad Heer Caalami Ah.

Barcelona ayaa ku guuldarreysatay muran dhanka sharciga ah oo kala dhexeeyay Zenit. Barcelona waxay ahayd inay hesho gunno marka Zenit ay u soo baxdo Champions League, waase haddii uu Malcom ciyaaro kala badh kulamadooda horyaalka. Si kastaba ha ahaatee, Zenit ayaa ku dooday in kooxaha Ruushka laga reebay tartanka Champions League kadib ganaaxii UEFA. Waxey Zenit ku andacootay in kooxaha Russia ay tahay in laga dhaafo lacagaha gunnada ah. Maxkamadda Xalinta Khilaafaadka Ciyaaraha (CAS) ayaa u xukuntay Zenit in laga cafiyo lacagtii €490, 000 ee gunnada Barcelona ay heli laheyd. Dhanka kale, Barcelona ayaa lagu wadaa inay FIFA ka hesho 6.92 milyan oo Yuuro oo magdhow ahaan loo siin doono Gavi kadib dhaawacii soo gaadhay intii uu u ciyaarayay Spain. FIFA waxay magdhow siisaa naadiyada dhaawacyada soo gaara inta lagu guda jiro waajibaadka caalamiga ah, ilaa lacag dhan 7.5 milyan euro sanadkii. Magdhawga dhaawaca Gavi ayaa u dhow xadkan, maadaama uu garoomada ka maqnaa ku dhawaad ​​mudadii xaq u lahaanshiyaha lacagta magdhowga. Barcelona ayaa aad u xiiseyneyso ku soo laabashada Gavi ee garoomada, maadaama joogistiisu ay si weyn u saameyn karto waxqabadkooda tartamada gudaha iyo Yurub labadaba.


Mourinho Oo Xiiseynaya Inuu Lasoo Wareego Xiddig Manchester United Kamid Ah.

Tababarihii hore ee Manchester United Jose Mourinho oo haatan tababare u ah kooxda Fenerbahce ee dalka Turkiga ayaa lagu soo waramayaa inuu xiiseynayo saxiixa daafaca kooxda Man United Victor Lindelof inta lagu guda jiro suuqa kala iibsiga bisha Janaayo. Lindelof, oo uu Mourinho keenay Manchester United 2017, ayaan hadda boos joogto ah ka heysan tababaraha hadda Manchester United Erik ten Hag. United ayaa isku dayday inay iska iibiso xiddigan xagaagii, laakiin dhaawac soo gaaray xiddiga ayaa u ogolaan weysay kooxda iney suuqa dhigto. Lindelof ayaa ku soo laabtay ciyaaraha waxyar ka hor fasaxa caalamiga ah ee October, laakiin United ayaan qorsheyneyn inay qandaraaska u cusbooneysiiso xiddiga xilli ciyaareedkan kadib. Mourinho ayaa ka fikiraya inuu u dhaqaaqo Lindelof bisha Janaayo si uu u xoojiyo difaaca Fenerbahce. Ma cadda in Mourinho uu doonayo in Lindelof uu isla markiiba ku biiro Fenerbahce ama uu sugo ilaa inta uu qandaraaskiisu dhamaanayo xagaaga. Ciyaartoyda uu qandaraaskoodu ka harsan yahay lix bilood ama wax kayar waxay la xaajoon karaan kooxaha ay ka helayaan xiisaha, laakiin waa inay sugaan ilaa inta uu qandaraaskooda ka dhamaanayo si ay koox kale ugu wareegaan. Zlatan Ibrahimovic ayaa sidoo kale lagu soo waramayaa inuu xiiseynayo inuu Lindelof keeno AC Milan, isagoo Mourinho kula tartamaya daafaca Swedishka ah.
